How to hide cables on a small desk

Cable management is the foundation of any truly minimalist workspace. When working with limited desk real estate, every exposed wire detracts from your focus and creates visual clutter. The secret lies in mounting your power strip directly beneath the desk surface using heavy-duty double-sided tape or screws, keeping the bulky plugs completely hidden from view.

Next, route your peripheral cables along the monitor arm or bind them together using simple velcro ties. For smaller items like charging cables and USB hubs, utilizing dedicated small desk organizers is essential to keep them accessible yet out of sight. If you are looking to elevate your setup, exploring minimalist mini setup ideas can provide you with the perfect inspiration to maintain a sleek, serene, and highly functional workspace without the chaos of tangled cords.
