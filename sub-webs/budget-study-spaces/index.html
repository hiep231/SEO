Optimizing Small Dorm Spaces

When you're living in a cramped dorm, vertical space is your best friend. Instead of laying everything out on your desk, use the walls! Pegboards, floating shelves, and monitor mounts free up crucial surface area for your actual textbooks and laptop.

Keep your color palette consistent to make the space feel less chaotic. By organizing your cables and picking multipurpose accessories, you can create a highly productive zone without breaking the bank.
