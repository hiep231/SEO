Buying Guide: Why Compact is King

Full-sized keyboards take up vital desk real estate, forcing your mouse arm into an uncomfortable outward angle. By switching to a 65% or 75% layout, you bring your mouse closer to your body's centerline, drastically reducing shoulder strain during long gaming or coding sessions.

A smaller keyboard also aesthetically transforms your workspace, leaving room for a cleaner setup. When pairing these keyboards with a clean desk, incorporating compact mini setup accessories ensures your wires are hidden and your peripherals shine, creating a futuristic, distraction-free environment.
